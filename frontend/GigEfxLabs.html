About Us

At GigEfx, we believe that Africa’s greatest challenges present opportunities for transformative innovation. Rooted in a deep understanding of local needs, we develop purpose-driven solutions that align with the realities of Africa’s 1.35 billion people—80% of whom remain underserved. As a startup studio, we ideate, develop, fund, and support ventures that address hundreds of pain points through our best-in-class innovation funnels.

Our approach is data-driven, empathetic, and collaborative, ensuring that every idea we generate is viable, desirable, and sustainable. We partner with like-minded entities, test innovations in real-world settings, and deploy solutions that can scale. By focusing on Africa’s growing middle class—expected to spend over $400 million daily—we establish a trusted supply value chain, bridging the gap between lower and higher socio-economic classes.

At the heart of GigEfx are our core values: accountability, integrity, and determination. Our innovation process is fueled by customer insights, allowing us to develop high-impact, inclusive, and cost-effective solutions. One of our pioneering ventures, Rapid Forge Solutions, leverages AI/ML-powered on-demand 3D printing services, revolutionizing fabrication accessibility across industries. As we continue to push boundaries, our mission remains clear: to design, test, and develop revenue-generating business models that compete with the best in Africa and beyond.
